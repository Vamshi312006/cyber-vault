# Purpose

Cyber Act is the engineering curriculum that powers this career system.

Its objective is not to complete topics.

Its objective is to transform engineering problems into practical capability through structured learning, implementation, and continuous reinforcement.

Every module should produce:

- Knowledge
- Engineering judgement
- Practical implementation
- Portfolio evidence
- Interview readiness

---

# What Cyber Act Teaches

Cyber Act follows the lifecycle of modern security engineering.

Secure

↓

This mirrors how real-world systems evolve.

---

# Curriculum Principles

Every stage follows these principles.

1. Learn the problem before the solution.

2. Understand the technology landscape before selecting one implementation.

3. Build one implementation deeply.

4. Understand competing approaches.

5. Every engineering decision must be justified.

6. Every module contributes to a flagship project.

7. Every module produces portfolio evidence.

8. Reading alone is never completion.

9. Implementation is mandatory.

10. Every stage ends with review and integration.

11. Progress is measured by engineering capability.

---

# Engineering Philosophy

Every module answers five questions.

What problem exists?

↓

What solutions exist?

↓

Why was one chosen?

↓

How is it implemented?

↓

Can I defend this decision?

If these cannot be answered, the module is incomplete.
