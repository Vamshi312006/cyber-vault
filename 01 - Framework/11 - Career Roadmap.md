> Status: Active
> Version: 1.0

# Purpose

This roadmap transforms long-term career goals into an executable plan.

It defines the major phases of development from the present day until placement readiness.

The roadmap focuses on engineering capability rather than completing arbitrary courses or collecting certificates.

---

# Final Objective

Become placement-ready for roles in:

- Application Security Engineering
- Detection Engineering
- Security Engineering
- Blue Team Engineering

through demonstrable engineering capability, flagship projects, and consistent execution.

# Guiding Principles

Always prioritize:

1. Skill development
2. Engineering quality
3. Consistent execution
4. Portfolio evidence
5. Interview readiness

Avoid prioritizing:

- Course completion
- Certificate collection
- Technology hopping
- Unnecessary redesign

---

# Success Criteria

The roadmap is complete when:

✓ Flagship projects are production quality.

✓ Core engineering skills are placement ready.

✓ Portfolio demonstrates practical capability.

✓ Resume accurately reflects engineering work.

✓ Interview performance consistently demonstrates technical confidence.

---

# Final Principle

The destination is not merely employment.

The destination is becoming an engineer capable of designing, building, securing, detecting, and continuously improving real-world systems.
